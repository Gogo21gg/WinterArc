7-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Full Body Workout

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Dumbbell Thrusters (or any heavy object): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Cardio

  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Shadow Boxing: 3 sets of 2 minutes
  • Jump Rope (or pretend to jump rope): 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Running in Place: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Stair Climbing (up and down a set of stairs): 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 3: Upper Body

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dips (using a chair): 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Incline Push-ups (feet on the ground, hands elevated): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Plank Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 10-15 reps (each side)
  • Superman Exercise: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bent-over Dumbbell Rows (using water bottles): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Wall Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Handstand Holds (against a wall): 3 sets of 20-30 seconds

Day 4: Lower Body

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Step-ups (on a sturdy chair): 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Wall Sits: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Side Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Donkey Kicks: 3 sets of 12-15 reps (each leg)

Day 5: Core Workout

  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (each side)
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (each side)
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 20-30 seconds (each side)
  • Flutter Kicks: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • V-Ups: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 6: Full Body HIIT

  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Plank Jacks: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Alternating Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Star Jumps: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Day 7: Recovery Day

  • Yoga or Stretching: 30 minutes
  • Light Walking: 20-30 minutes
